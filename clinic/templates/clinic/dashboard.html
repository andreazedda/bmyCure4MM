What are assessments? Medical evaluations tracking patient progress over time.

Key Metrics Explained:

  • M-Protein: Abnormal protein produced by myeloma cells (lower is better, normal: 0 g/dL)

  • Response: How well treatment is working (CR=Complete Response, PR=Partial Response, etc.)
