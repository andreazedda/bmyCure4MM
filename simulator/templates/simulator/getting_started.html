üî¨ What happens behind the scenes:

Think of a mathematical model like a weather forecast, but for cancer treatment. Just as meteorologists use equations to predict rain, we use equations to predict how tumor cells and healthy cells respond to drugs over time.

Our model includes:

Il nostro modello include:

  • Cell growth equations: How fast cells divide
  • Drug effects: How medicines kill cancer cells
  • Side effects: Impact on healthy cells
  • Competition: Tumor cells vs healthy cells
  • Equazioni crescita cellulare: Quanto velocemente si dividono le cellule
  • Effetti farmaci: Come le medicine uccidono le cellule tumorali
  • Effetti collaterali: Impatto su cellule sane
  • Competizione: Cellule tumorali vs cellule sane
